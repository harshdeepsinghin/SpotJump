Features

YouTube to Spotify

Adds a "SpotJump" button next to the Like/Dislike buttons on YouTube. Click it to instantly search for the video on Spotify.

Spotify to YouTube

Adds a "Jump to YouTube" button (play icon) in the Spotify Web Player. Click it to find the current song on YouTube.

Why SpotJump?

SpotJump bridges the gap between your favorite music platforms. Whether you've discovered a new track on YouTube and want to save it to your Spotify library, or you're listening on Spotify and want to watch the music video on YouTube, SpotJump makes the transition seamless.
